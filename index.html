This site is a long-term audit of everything I find interesting enough to take apart. I apply it to medieval literature, sacred texts, political monsters, belief systems in freefall, and cult leaders who built empires out of other people's fear. Then I add a second lens. Sometimes a third. The lenses are chosen the same way the subjects are chosen — because the combination produces a finding nobody was expecting. The methodology stays constant. The lenses rotate. The texts don't get to opt out.
