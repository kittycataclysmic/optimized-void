WHAT THIS IS

This is not a book review site. There are no stars, no recommendations, no reading lists. This is a process audit — applied to literature that was never asking to be audited. Every text gets the same treatment: four questions, five lenses, one verdict. The goal is not to celebrate these works. The goal is to understand why the systems they describe failed, who they failed, and whether anyone designed them to fail on purpose.

THE FIVE LENSES — APPLIED IN ORDER

LENS 01 LSS Audit

What is the process. Where is the waste. What is the failure mode. The constitution — present in every project on this site.

LENS 02 Foucault

What does this system normalize. What does it make invisible. Who built the conditions that made everything else inevitable.

LENS 03 Nietzsche

Who is asserting will and to what end. What happens when the will to power meets a system that was never designed to contain it.

LENS 04 Jungian Archetypes

What roles are the players locked into. Who is the Shadow. What the system officially contains but structurally enables.

LENS 05 Cognitive Bias

What psychological mechanism ensures nobody escapes. The final audit finding — why the system perpetuates even after the defect is visible.
